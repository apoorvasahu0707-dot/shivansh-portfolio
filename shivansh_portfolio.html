About me

From farm floor
to farm future

I'm Shivansh Mishra — a mushroom cultivation specialist and farm operations lead at Labh Earth. My work sits at the intersection of applied biology, agricultural engineering, and practical agribusiness.

I don't just grow mushrooms. I design the environments, build the systems, write the SOPs, and troubleshoot everything in between — so that farms run reliably, not just once but batch after batch.

Whether it's designing a new fruiting chamber from scratch, diagnosing a contamination problem, or helping a vendor partner set up their first oyster mushroom unit — I show up and figure it out.

I believe mushroom farming is one of India's most underrated agricultural opportunities — and I'm here to build the infrastructure that proves it.
